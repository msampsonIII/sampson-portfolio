Myles Sampson is a PhD Student studying Computational Design in the School of Architecture at Carnegie Mellon University. He was previously a Post-Graduate Teaching Fellow and Research Associate in the MIT Department of Architecture. He graduated with a Master of Science in Architectural Studies from the MIT Design and Computation Group in 2022. He is from Pensacola, Florida, and attended Tuskegee University, where he graduated with a professional degree in architecture in 2019. His research focuses on robotic architectural construction, where he investigates methods for automated construction processes.
